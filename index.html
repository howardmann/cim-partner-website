Why it's hard to cross alone

Firms that try to cross alone get stuck.

Building the platform a modern MBCx contract demands is a multi-year software job. It requires, minimum:

  1. 01A normalised data layer across every BMS protocol in your portfolio.
  2. 02A tagging and modelling stack that doesn't need hand-assembly on every site.
  3. 03A fault detection library battle-tested across thousands of assets.
  4. 04An alert-to-action workflow your clients will actually live inside.
  5. 05An AI investment budget that keeps compounding while your competitors catch up.
A tagging framework is not a platform. A rules editor is not a delivered capability. A data layer is not a platform either. What a modern services firm needs is a delivered, managed capability their team walks into on day one.
